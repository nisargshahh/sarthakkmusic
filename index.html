Bombay Mafia LP

Bombay Mafia, is an LP by Sarthak, which dropped on 13th April 2022. The project was long awaited by the Listeners and it did create that hype. The whole project shifts genres and styles throughout And is not based on one beat style or vocal style, Bombay Mafia is a complete Packet of Music with a lot of Variations and features a lot of fellow artists from the Scene. Bombay Mafia is a great musical project, and there are Songs of every style, and after listening to it for once you Will fall in love with this beautiful project.

The LP consists of 20 tracks, and our favourites from Them are: Kaali Duniya, Kaali Gaadi, Raabta, Killing My Demons, Meherbani. The artists lined up through the LP are: Roygin, Vasudev, Aardicy, Rakht, Genesis, Smrutika, 2facebleed, Bias, Ayaan, KK, Shatam, Sane, Addyksn, Siege, Mark Bhatia. These were just the vocalists, but Sarthak got a good Lineup of producers for his LP, Archit Tak, Vasudev, Electrona, Wamp, Autumn Beatz, Ajinkya Bakshi, Captain Pune, Vibhakar, Kahanikaar were the Producers who Gave the magical sound to Bombay Mafia LP.

It is a Must listen, and you all should check It right away.
