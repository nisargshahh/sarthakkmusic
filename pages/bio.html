About $arthak

$arthak AKA Sarthak Pattnaik is an indian Hip hop artist, who likes to make hip hop music with indian music consciousness. Being one of the most versatile in the scene, creates a different channel for him to move on it. $arthak has dropped 2 projects i.e Monsoon cassia the mixtape & Bombay mafia the Long Play. Talking about getting featured on big platform's, $arthak has been featured on rolling stone, indie radio & livelee. Prestigious news Channel like Apn news, have also covered his journey &his hustle. Being young & carrying a different sound enchances his music & creation technique. By being more inclined to his art, $arthak proves his dedication & determination for his music. He will surely continue to do so//.
